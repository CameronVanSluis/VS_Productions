About

Biography

My name is Cameron van Sluis, and I'm a Digital Content Creator from Vancouver Washington. I specialize in Video Production & Motion Graphics, but also have experience in Graphic Design and Web Design.

I love to express myself through story and to create unique viewing experiences. Video is my favorite creative medium because it allows me to do that within the digital realm. Entertaining the audience is top priority when it comes to my creative practice. I strongly believe that great audio is imperative to producing great videos. That’s why I utilize my experience in sound design and mixing to incorporate an “audio first” approach to many of my projects.
